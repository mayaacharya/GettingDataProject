##Codebook

Subjects: 
	Subjects are numbered from 1 to 30
Motions: 
	WALKING
	WALKING_UPPSTAIRS
	WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
	SITTING
	STANDING
	LAYING
Data:
	mean --> average for that data
	std --> standard deviation for that data

Units:
	accelaration data is in standard gravitational units 'g'
	rotational data is in radians per second
